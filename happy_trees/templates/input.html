Welcome to Happy Little Trees!

This app will help you select the best colors to paint the image of your choice! It will give you the exact Daniel Smith tube color and mixing recipes to make the same color with a professionally selected 3-color palette.


Mixing your own colors with a limited palette will make your painting more harmonious and will help avoid muddy colors and can save you money!
